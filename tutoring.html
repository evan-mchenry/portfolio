Tutoring

From directing my high school’s writing center to teaching at Choate Rosemary Hall, I’ve always been committed to helping students excel.

I graduated Phi Beta Kappa from Brown University with degrees in English and Literary Arts. My essays have won the Preston Gurney Fiction Prize and the Preston Gurney Poetry Prize at Brown, and I was selected as the Regional Winner in Literature for the U.S. and Canada in the Global Undergraduate Awards. My creative writing has been published in The Onion.

I have experience working for multiple highly rated tutoring companies and now offer independent tutoring and editing services for new students!
